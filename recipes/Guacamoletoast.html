Guacamole Toast

Description

An easy, quick, nutritious, breakfast meal

Your wife will love you!

Ingredients

  • Avocado
  • Bread preferably rye
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Jalapeno peppers (optional)
  • Sesame oil

Steps

  1. Toast your bread
  2. Mash your Avocado
  3. Spread your mashed Avocado on the toast
  4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
  5. Server immediately
