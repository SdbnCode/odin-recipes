Lasagna

Description

A pasta dish with layers of delicious meat sauce and cheese

Your friends will love it and you even more!

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Basil
  • Sheets of pasta

Steps

  1. Purchase all of your ingredients
  2. Make the meat sauce
  3. Cook the noodles
  4. Layer the lasagna with one layer of meat sauce
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
