Mac and Cheese

Description

A simple easy dish to pair with any BBQ

Quick and simple, your guest will love you!

Ingredients

  • Macaroni pasta
  • American Cheese, quartered
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook your Macaroni
  2. Pre-heat your oven to 120 degrees celsius
  3. Put your Macaroni into a baking tray
  4. Throw in American Cheese
  5. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
  6. Place your baking tray in the oven for about 1 1/2 hours and serve while hot
